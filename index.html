Overview

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and other electronic devices from unauthorized access, theft, damage, and other malicious activities. This includes a range of strategies, such as implementing firewalls and encryption, performing regular software updates and backups, and educating users on safe browsing and email practices. Cybersecurity is an increasingly important issue in today's digital age, as more and more sensitive information is stored and transmitted online.

Failure to properly secure these systems can result in devastating consequences, including financial loss, identity theft, and damage to reputation.With the increasing use of technology and the internet, cybersecurity has become a critical concern for individuals, businesses, and governments around the world.

On this website, I hope to provide resources that explain what Cybersecurity is, how it might impact you, how you can be more secure online, and provide up-to-date news and events from the Cybersecurity world.
